About Meri Mitti

Our story, our mission, and the people behind the brand

Meri Mitti was born from a simple but powerful belief — that the world deserves access to truly pure, organic, and desi food, sourced with honesty and care. From the rich soils of Jhalawar, Rajasthan, we set out to connect conscious consumers across the globe with the finest organic produce from small farmers everywhere.

🌍 Our Mission

To take the pure world forward on the path of organic living and healthcare. We believe that real food — grown with love, free from chemicals, rooted in ancient wisdom — is the foundation of a healthy world. Our mission is to make this available to every person on this planet, regardless of where they live.

Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated: March 2026 — Meri Mitti, Jhalawar, Rajasthan, India

At Meri Mitti, your privacy and security are our top priorities. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. Founded by Lakshit Gauttam & Chunav Sharma, we are committed to full transparency.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address when you create an account or place an order
  • Shipping address and payment information (payment details are encrypted and never stored)
  • Communication history when you contact our support team
  • Usage data to improve your shopping experience

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To send order confirmations and tracking updates
  • To send newsletters (only if you subscribed — you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To improve our website and product offerings
  • To prevent fraud and ensure account security

3. OTP & Email Verification

Meri Mitti uses OTP (One-Time Password) verification via email to ensure account security. Your email is never shared with any third party. OTPs expire within 10 minutes for your protection.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure payment gateways, and regular security audits. Your personal data is stored on secure servers and is never sold to advertisers or third parties.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and download your personal data at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Contact us at [email protected] for any privacy concerns

6. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at [email protected] or visit our Contact page.
